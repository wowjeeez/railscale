## Train related words

- **Carriage** L5+ networking concepts http, tls, grpc etc 
– *travelling across the railway in a certain shape*
- **Passenger** a Frame implementation
- **Freight** Unbuffered body
- **Railway** network itself (2+ interconnected points)
- **Locomotive** L4 network implementations (TCP/UDP/QUIC)
- **Station** network member (N/host)
- **Yard** a localized cluster of stations either running ipc or independently (1/host)
- **Conductor** builder&factory implementations
- **Inspector** logging, pcap and utilities
- **Shuttle** peering/bridging between railscale and another service (e.g tailscale)
- **Governor** control plane
